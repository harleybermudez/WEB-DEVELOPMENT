iPhone 17 - Technical Specifications

Processor & Memory

  • Chip: Apple A19 Bionic Chip (3 nm)
  • CPU: Hexa-core (6-core: 2 Performance, 4 Efficiency)
  • GPU: 5-core Apple GPU with Neural Accelerators
  • RAM: 8GB LPDDR5X
  • Storage Options (NVMe): 256GB, 512GB

Display

  • Type: Super Retina XDR OLED, ProMotion (1-120Hz Adaptive)
  • Size: 6.3 inches (Diagonal)
  • Resolution: 2622 x 1206 pixels, 460 ppi
  • Protection: Ceramic Shield 2, Anti-reflective coating
  • Max Brightness: 1000 nits (Typical), 3000 nits (Peak Outdoor)

Camera System

Camera Resolution / Aperture Key Features
Main (Wide) 48MP, ƒ/1.6 Sensor-shift OIS, Dual Pixel PDAF, Photonic Engine
Ultra Wide 48MP, ƒ/2.2 (120° FOV) Macro photography support, Hybrid Focus Pixels
Front (Selfie) 18MP, ƒ/1.9 Autofocus with Focus Pixels, Center Stage
Video Recording 4K up to 60 fps (Dolby Vision HDR) Spatial Video, Dual Capture, Cinematic Mode

Connectivity & Power

  • Cellular & Wireless: 5G, Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be), Bluetooth 6.0, Thread
  • Port: USB-C 2.0 (Supports USB 2 speeds)
  • Battery: Li-Ion 3692 mAh (Up to 30 hours video playback)
  • Charging: 25W Fast Charging (50% in 20 min), 15W MagSafe/Qi2 Wireless
  • Dimensions: 149.6 x 71.5 x 8.0 mm (5.89 x 2.81 x 0.31 in)
  • Weight: 177 g (6.24 oz)
  • Durability: IP68 (Max depth of 6 meters for up to 30 minutes)
